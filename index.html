hello there,

we are bullfinch

giving a voice to the ordinary idiots like us

we are a small press (bull) & literary magazine (finch) based in San Francisco. we appreciate niche publishers, niche writers, and nice readers—they’re doing an outstanding job advocating for niche content. but our approach to publishing is more egalitarian. you won’t find mission statements here. we aren’t committed to women over men. we aren’t interested in one political ideology over another. we don’t prefer “experimental” over literary fiction. we care about great writing. you’re absolutely encouraged to shock us—but we’d also like to be comforted, uplifted, energized, delighted, bewildered. we’re interested in writing that makes us feel. if your writing does that, it doesn’t matter who you are—there is a place for you here. our submission process is 100% blind, and we’ll publish what we love without regard to who or where it comes from.

MEDIA

we've got our feathers in all kinds of cool things

finch MAGAZINE

our online literary magazine publishes prose—fiction and nonfiction. we’re not especially preoccupied with genre. our goal is to publish engaging work—no matter who wrote it, or why. new, emerging, and established writers welcome here.

BLOG

READING SERIES

bullfinch’s guided reading series is offered on a seasonal basis—like a book club, but better. because you don’t have to show up or bring snacks you are only pretending to have made. we alternate selections, so every other month will be a pretty terrible book. but you have to read it, and we're not telling you which month is the bad one.
